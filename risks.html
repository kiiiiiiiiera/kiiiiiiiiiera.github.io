Technology Risks

Potential risks which could be created by keeping the internet open are:

RISKS: Internet terrorism/ spread of rumors

  • Cyberterrorism
  • Usually has the goal to promote the attackers political, religious or social issue (Akhgar, et al. 2014).
  • Can cause government attacks (Andress & Winterfeld, 2013).

RISK: Data mining

  • They use software to find patterns in the user’s data (Twin, 2021).
  • Can be corrupt

RISK: Harmful content(Lu et al., 2016).

  • Able to forbid content which could be harmful to internet users(Lu et al., 2016)
  • Beneficial to users to have censorship
  • Easier to catch illegal activity
