Technology Choices

Possible choices which could be adopted while keeping the internet open are:

CHOICES: Strengthen social supervision for the environment

  • Develop and publish open government data
  • Strengthen the propaganda of open government data
  • Inspire more public oversight

CHOICES: Establish an assessment mechanism for effective organisation

  • Assessment content
  • Corresponding reward and punishment mechanism

CHOICES: Reduce the risks to the individual of open internet

  • Institutions construction for open government data
  • Building a data security protection system

From looking at the opportunities and risks, we have come to a conclusion on what we believe is the best solution to this issue. We believe that for internet censorship in China, the ideal solution is to lessen the restrictions online to allow the public to know what occurs on a global level and not just in China. They are currently isolated from the world and therefore, are unable to form any opinion on matters. There are a few possible choices which could be adopted to create this situation. One option is to strengthen the social supervision for the online environment. This is the idea of creating more trust between the government and users. This can be strengthened by making the government look trustworthy to the public. Some examples on how trust can be built is through an open government. An open government will allow citizens to have a greater awareness of what the government does (Opendatasoft, 2021). Censorship is the repression of speech through suppressing and deleting anything considered objectionable to the state. Censorship is used by countries throughout the world, regardless of their regime types or developmental levels.
